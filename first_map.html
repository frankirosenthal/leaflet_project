Best Places to Visit While in Greece

This map shows some of the amazing places to visit in Greece. Greece is a country in southeastern Europe known for its beautiful islands and ancient history. Its capital city, Athens, is known for landmarks such as the Acropolis and the Parthenon. Greece is also admired for its many beaches, most notably on the island of Santorini and the island of Mykonos.
